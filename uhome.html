BRIEF HISTORY


UNASHAMED CHARITY FOUNDATION

Unashamed Charity Group was formed in 2019 by a Group of Scholars who were the then Form Four leavers of Nkaimurunya Secondary School. The team being led by David Irihose (founder and president of Unashamed Charity Group) had the urge to retain the unity of youths and his Classmates purposing to serve God and the society as well. The continued pressures and urge to be united , to serve God and the society, gave rise to a YOUTHS CHARITY MOVEMENT which came to be known as UNASHAMED CHARITY GROUP. The name “UNASHAMED” is derived from Romans 1:16 “For I am not ashamed of the Gospel of Christ: for it’s the power of God unto salvation to everyone that believeth; to the Jews first, and also to the Greeks.
